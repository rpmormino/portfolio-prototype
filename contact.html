hello!

My name is Rose Michael P. Mormino, but I just go by Rose. I was born and raised in North Carolina, where I attended UNC Greensboro for my undergraduate degree. For the past few years, I have been pursuing further education in the areas of computer science, web development, and web design.


I’ve always been driven to create art; when I was younger, I drew comics, wrote newspapers, drafted fantasy maps, and penned plays. In middle school and high school, I shifted more toward character driven art, building worlds and filling them with original characters and creatures. At university, I earned a degree in Theatre, with a concentration in technical production and design.


As part of my coursework for my advanced web design certificate, I am running a weekly blog with content relating to my field—web design, front end development, UI/UX design, and graphic design. Through this blog I aim to learn even more about the field and find intersections with my other hobbies and interests, as well as meet and connect with others interested in design.
